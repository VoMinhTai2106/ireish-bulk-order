Common Questions

Q1 How is the discount for a bulk order calculated?

Our pricing is tiered, meaning the more you order, the greater the discount per item. The custom quote in your request will include an estimated savings comparison versus standard pricing, but the exact discount can vary by product type and design complexity. We'll provide a firm per‚Äëunit price and total savings once we review your specific order request.

Q2 How long will my bulk order take to produce?

Turnaround time depends on the product and quantity. Our support team will provide you with a detailed production timeline along with your quote.

Q3 Can I mix and match different items in my bulk order?

Absolutely! You can combine multiple product lines in one bulk order. Please note that discounts may differ across item types‚Äîmixing products may result in different savings compared to ordering a single product lines at volume.

Q4 What customization options are available?

We offer custom Greek letters, chapter names, founding dates, colors, and exclusive designs tailored to your organization. We can also create custom designs based on your specific requests.

Q5 What is your return/refund policy for bulk orders?

Because bulk orders are fully customized, they are non-refundable/returnable. However, we stand by our quality. If your order arrives with a manufacturing defect or does not match the approved proof, we will work with you to reprint and correct the issue at our expense.

Q6 What if I need to change or cancel my order?

You can make changes to your order at any point before you approve the final proof. Once the proof is approved and the order is sent to production, we can no longer make changes or cancellations. Please review your proof carefully before giving your approval.
